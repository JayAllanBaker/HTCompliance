Here is a crisp, “Measure What Matters” style OKR set for your compliance application. Timeframe: Q1 FY26. North Star metric: Percent of obligations met on time.

Objective 1: Eliminate missed filings and payments across states

Key Results:

Raise On-Time Compliance Rate across state filings and payments from baseline to 99 percent

Cut late fees to zero dollars for the quarter

Auto schedule at least 95 percent of recurring filings and remittances with owner and due date set

Reduce average lead time from first alert to completion from baseline to three days

Primary initiatives (not scored): build deadline calendar with jurisdiction rules, SLA timers, and retries. Add exception queue and one click remediation.

Objective 2: Make payroll compliance fully reliable

Key Results:

Complete 100 percent of payroll cycles on the planned date with proof of transmission

Achieve zero payroll tax penalties and zero interest assessments

Reach 100 percent reconciliation of payroll tax liabilities to payments within two business days

Reduce manual steps per payroll run to three or fewer for the operator of record

Primary initiatives: connectors to payroll provider and bank, automated payment files, return file ingestion, variance flags.

Objective 3: Turn contracts into executable obligations

Key Results:

Ingest 100 percent of active contracts with structured metadata for term, renewals, deliverables, and payment triggers

Ensure 100 percent of upcoming obligations have an owner, due date, and linked evidence location

Reduce missed contractual deliverables to zero

Link obligations to invoices or accrual entries for 90 percent of items due this quarter

Primary initiatives: contract parser, obligation template library, reminder ladder at 30, 14, and 3 days, billing linkage.

Objective 4: Be audit ready at any time

Key Results:

Generate a complete evidence pack for a quarterly audit in under two hours from push button start

Close evidence gaps to fewer than five items in the quarter

Pass quarterly backup and full restore test in under four hours with zero data loss

Reduce P0 compliance defects in production to zero

Primary initiatives: immutable audit trail, evidence registry, automated screenshots and exports, backup and restore drillbook.

Objective 5: Prove adoption and value

Key Results:

Reach five active tenants with at least three compliance domains turned on per tenant

Lift Weekly Active Users among operators to twenty across all pilots

Hit a Net Promoter Score of 40 or higher from operator users

Demonstrate a 50 percent reduction in manual tracking time for one pilot customer as measured by time study

Primary initiatives: onboarding playbooks, tenant templates, in app guides, value calculator.

Scoring rubric (per KR)

0.0: no progress
0.3: some progress toward baseline plus
0.7: substantial progress but short of target
1.0: target achieved or exceeded

Metric definitions

On-Time Compliance Rate: number of filings and obligations completed on or before due date divided by total due in period.
Lead Time: days from first system alert to completed evidence.
Evidence Pack: export of audit trail, approvals, artifacts, and reconciliations for the selected scope.
Manual Steps per Run: distinct human actions required to complete the cycle.

Weekly check-in template

Confidence: green or yellow or red
Progress this week: brief notes per objective
KR updates: current value vs target with quick comment
Risks and asks: blockers and decisions needed
Next week plan: top three moves
